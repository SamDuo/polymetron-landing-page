FAQ

Common questions

What types of development does Polymetron support?

Commercial, industrial, multifamily, data centers, logistics, mixed-use, and renewable energy infrastructure. Scoring weights adapt to each asset class.

How does AI risk scoring work?

Each parcel receives a 0–100 composite score evaluating flood risk, power access, climate hazards, zoning fit, and environmental constraints. Weights are configurable per investment thesis.

Can Polymetron integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. Exports to standard GIS formats, Excel, and PDF. API integrations with common CRE platforms and internal data pipelines.

How fast can we get started?

Parcel screening begins within days. Full deployment with custom scoring models typically takes two to four weeks.

What geographic areas does Polymetron cover?

Currently focused on major U.S. metros, with Atlanta as the primary pilot market. National coverage is expanding — contact us to discuss your target region.

What data sources power the scoring?

Parcel boundaries and zoning from county assessors, FEMA flood maps, utility infrastructure layers, EPA environmental data, and climate risk projections from peer-reviewed models.
